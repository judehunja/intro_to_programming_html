Chicken Curry Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Final Solution

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves of well-chopped garlic
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds.
  3. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and sauté for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes, coriander powder, turmeric powder, chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the oil separates from the mixture.
  6. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they turn white on all sides.
  7. Add enough water to cover the chicken and bring to a boil. Then, reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked.
  8. Stir in the garam masala and cook for an additional 5 minutes.

Final Solution

Your delicious chicken curry is ready to be served. Enjoy it with rice or ugali!

Additional information

Here are some tips for enhancing the flavor of your chicken curry:

  • Adjust the amount of spices according to your preference
  • You can add stuff like peas for extra flavor
